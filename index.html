ABOUT ROTATO
Since the debut of its first outlet in October 2022, Rotato has evolved from a single stall outlet into a multi-national food franchise. With more than 10 locations operating throughout Indonesia.

Potato Corner quickly established itself as the main seller of flavored french fries. One bite to Potato Corner BBQ flavored french fries and you will immediately understand why there is “a lot of shock happening!”
